# Meetings & Events Module
![meetings](/img/meetings.png)
## Purpose
This module is used to organize, schedule, and manage meetings and events related to constituency activities, enabling better tracking and coordination of engagements.
## How to Use
-	Click on “New Meeting” to create and schedule a meeting or event.
-	Enter required details such as title, date, time, format, and location.
-	Use the search bar and filters to find specific meetings. 
-	Switch between Grid and Table views to easily manage and review records.
